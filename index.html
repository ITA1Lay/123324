The Diploma in government investigation:

is designed for those engaged in investigative functions in the Australian or state public service.Among other duties, the task of an investigator is to collect, evaluate and use evidence to support litigation. The role of an investigator may be an exciting and intense profession.

Certificate IV in Work Health & Safety:

The course is specially designed and tailored for participants engaged in construction and construction, civil construction, engineering and railway projects. Health and safety officers can play various roles in helping organizations raise safety awareness and work with other employees to identify risks and hazards, solve problems and implement safety plans.

Certificate IV in Procurement and Contracting:

Psp40616 level IV procurement and contracting certificate is designed to be practical and provide you with the skills and knowledge you need to improve in the workplace. Our experienced trainers will help you fully master the basic elements of government procurement, contract management and how to purchase goods and services to meet business needs.

Diploma of Social Media Marketing:

The Diploma in social media marketing is an innovative social media marketing qualification designed by experts. It provides strategic training for the next generation of social media marketing managers and the tools needed to implement social media activities, advertising and media purchases and content marketing in traditional Facebook, LinkedIn and Youtube channels and emerging channels such as tiktok, snapchat and wechat.
